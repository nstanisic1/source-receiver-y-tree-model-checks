Table 9 summarizes the elevation-based visibility coverage underlying the Supplementary Figure S1 ancient-DNA directness and ecological visibility display. It is regenerated from the audited Supplementary Figure S1 row-level elevation, unique-site elevation, and direct-negative-control source rows. It compares period-relevant ancient-DNA sites, branch-derived PH908 relic-footprint localities, visible E-V13/J2b/R1b/G2a ancient-DNA comparator sites, and strict direct negative-control candidates.

PH908 relic-footprint localities are the fixed 11-row Supplementary Figure S1 target layer derived from branch-map rows passing parent-or-anchor age >=1400 ybp. `source_data/supplementary_figure_s1/s1_19_ph908_relic_footprint_derivation_trace.csv` documents the age-gated upstream layer, the ambiguous Y283553 exclusion, and the plotted-layer reconciliation. Elevation thresholds are descriptive coverage summaries and are not treated as evidence of PH908 presence, PH908 absence, geographic origin, ethnic identity, population continuity, or migration route. Period-relevant ancient-DNA sites are dated 2500 BCE-550 CE. Exclusion rules, branch-derived locality provenance, and directness criteria are defined in the Methods and S1 source bundle.

For the strict direct negative-control candidate row, the 800 m count is reported as zero because no candidate is at or above the lower 500 m threshold.
