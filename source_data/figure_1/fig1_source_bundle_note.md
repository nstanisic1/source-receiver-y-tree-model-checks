Figure 1 source-data bundle for the reliability-aware source–receiver model-checking framework.

This bundle reconstructs the existing Figure 1 framework schematic as a numbered, auditable source-data package. The figure is conceptual, but its displayed steps, output states, formulas, and claim-tiering language are encoded in CSV files rather than treated as untracked drawing annotations.

The framework begins with a specified source–receiver hypothesis and a public Y-tree branch-country observation layer. It then applies source–receiver geometry, where `A_s` denotes source adequacy and `D = ln(q2_receiver / q2_source)` denotes receiver/source Hill order-2 branch-diversity contrast. The reliability layer applies branch-unit sensitivity, country perturbation, rarefaction or minimum-data checks, and no-call rules before assigning one of four decision states: compatible state, stable model rejection, fragile/support-only state, or no-call.

Figure 1 defines the decision grammar used by the reported analysis. It is not a data-result figure and should not be interpreted as evidence of exact geographic origin, ethnic identity, population continuity, migration route, ancient-DNA confirmation, or population-level probability.
