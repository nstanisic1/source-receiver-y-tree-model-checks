# Data-Use Boundary

The code in `scripts/` is released under the MIT License unless otherwise stated in a specific file.

Repository documentation, figures, tables, source-data bundles and derived aggregate data are provided to support transparency and reproduction of the stated analyses. They are not relicensed by the MIT code license.

Raw upstream resources and third-party public-tree, project-register and ancient-DNA materials remain subject to their source providers, publications, citation requirements and terms of use. This repository does not redistribute raw genetic sequence files, YFull user-level records, private sample identifiers, kit numbers, tester names, surnames, usernames, email addresses, payment information or per-sample geographic annotations.

The Natural Earth administrative-boundary layer bundled for Supplementary Fig. 1 rendering is public-domain map data and is not a genetic or public-tree user-level source.

The repository contains aggregate source-data layers sufficient to regenerate the reported figures, tables, manifests and numerical checks. These layers support reproducibility of the specified source-receiver model checks; they are not population-representative samples and should not be interpreted as evidence of geographic origin, ethnic identity, population continuity, migration route, ancient-DNA confirmation or population-level probability.
